The Gym.

Bouldering.

A unique style of climbing done over a padded floor without ropes or harnesses. You can boulder with or without a partner.

Ropes.

Top-rope and auto-belay climbing are done with a rope suspended overhead — top-roping needs a partner to belay, auto-belays don't. Lead climbing is advanced and starts with the rope at the bottom, clipping to progressive protection points as you ascend.

Yoga.

Beyond the walls, LEF runs a regular yoga schedule — a chance to stretch out, build mobility, and recover between climbs. Check the calendar and sign up through the portal.

Learn the ropes.

New here? A few classes get you climbing safely and confidently — from your very first session onward.

Climbing 101

The foundation class.

Whether you're chasing your first send or building a foundation for the long haul, our Climbing 101 clinic is the place to start.

A four-week clinic for brand-new and improving climbers alike, taught by CWI-certified instructors. Gear rentals and a 30-day membership are included, so you have everything you need to keep climbing between sessions.

  1. Gym navigation — etiquette and the safety fundamentals.
  2. Movement — crossing, flagging, and reading routes.
  3. Harder problems — managing energy and pushing past plateaus.
  4. Your training plan — built around your goals.

Top Rope Class

If you haven't belayed before — or it's been a while — the top-rope class covers everything you need to safely belay a partner on our top-rope walls. Just want to climb? Show up for a quick auto-belay or bouldering orientation instead.

Learn to Lead

Ready to move past top-rope? Learn to Lead covers clipping, slack management, and catching falls so you can start lead climbing in the gym. Take your time getting comfortable before you join us above the bolt.

Good to know.

Who can climb with us?

Climbing is an athletic endeavor, but we try to make our product as accessible as possible. If you're fit enough to climb a ladder you can definitely climb with us! Auto belays do have a physical weight range of 25–310lbs. Children under 14 years of age must be supervised.

Do I have to be experienced?

Not at all, though we do have challenges that are appropriate for any experience level. If you're not finding something that works for you, our route setting team is friendly and willing to work something out!

What if I need help?

If you're looking for help during a casual day of climbing we can arrange for a coach to join you. We do need at least a week of notice to schedule, and you will need to pay our private instruction rates for the service. We also offer group instruction, social events, youth programs, and clinics!

Can I have a party or event?

We have all you need to host a birthday party, event, or team building opportunity! Larger groups will require a bit more notice, so don't hesitate to reach out and get things started as soon as possible!
