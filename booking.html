How our pricing works.

All group packages start at a base rate of $35 per person and scale on three factors:

  1. Group size. Larger groups benefit from volume discounts.
  2. Duration. Most sessions run 2 to 3 hours, and can extend to full days on request.
  3. Resources. Higher tiers add dedicated instructors, facilitators, exclusive wall access, and other amenities.

What every group receives.

  • Safety orientation and basic equipment instruction for everyone
  • All necessary climbing gear — harness and shoes
  • Dedicated check-in and group coordination on arrival
  • Access to restrooms, water, and common areas
  • Waivers handled digitally in advance to speed up arrival
  • Most groups are expected to be fairly self-sufficient; if you'd like more one-on-one help, tell us in advance — fees apply.
