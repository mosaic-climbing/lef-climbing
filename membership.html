Memberships.

EFT Membership.

Recurring monthly billing. Best if you'll be a member for two or more months.

Unlimited climbing on the full LEF facility — bouldering, top-rope, lead, and auto-belay. Member-only access from 10am to 12pm daily, plus full open hours.

Adult (14+)$85 / month
Youth (0–13)$55 / month
Couple (two adults)$153 / month
Youth added to a couple+ $45 / month

Family pricing applies when youth are added to an adult or couple plan. Sales tax calculated at the time of billing.

30-day Prepaid.

One up-front payment for 30 days. No recurring billing.

Good for short stays, visitors, or trying the gym before signing up for an EFT membership.

Adult (14+)$100 / 30 days
Youth (0–13)$65 / 30 days

Plus sales tax. Each participant priced individually — no couple discount on prepaid.
